OPERATION CHANGE

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What is Operation Change?

OPERATION CHANGE is a movement designed to inspire others to take action to help make the world a better place. It has garnered support from some of the world’s most powerful celebrities, influential leaders and like-minded charities.

The work of OPERATION CHANGE served as the foundation for a 10-part documentary series that aired on OWN: Oprah Winfrey Network. In the series, Team OPERATION CHANGE traveled the world to bring hope to people and communities with seemingly insurmountable challenges.

For information aboutOPERATION CHANGE, please visit the About OPERATION CHANGE page within the website.

What can I do on the website?

Hear the stories of the lives that are touched and see power of people coming together by watching all 10 commercial-free OPERATION CHANGE episodes, viewing exclusive clips, exploring information about the countries featured and sharing content with friends via social media platforms.

In addition, when watching the OPERATION CHANGE episodes, you can enhance your experience by accessing the story mode feature. By simply clicking on the Story Mode button that automatically appears with the episode, you will be provided with additional content related to the episode, such as: cultural facts, virtual tours and quotes and blog posts from the OPERATION CHANGE team members who experienced it all first-hand.

By getting an OPERATION CHANGE Passport, you will automatically earn points when consuming content and have the chance to be featured on the homepage.

How do I get my OPERATION CHANGE Passport?

Getting your Passport is easy! Do so by either creating an OPERATION CHANGE account using your email address or sign in via Facebook

To get started, simply click on the LOG IN / JOIN US link in the upper right-hand corner or click on the Passport image featured on the homepage.

How do I earn points?

OPERATION CHANGE Passport holders will automatically receive points for watching video content on the website when logged in. Your points will be tracked, and the top 3 point earners will be featured on the OPERATION CHANGE website homepage for all to see!

How can I get involved?

The goal of OPERATION CHANGE is to inspire others to take action in an effort to help make the world a better place. Sharing OPERATION CHANGE website content with friends, volunteering with a charity in your own community or engaging in a random act of kindness are all examples of ways to get involved. You don’t have to travel the world to make a difference. A single caring gesture can have an positive impact beyond measure.

If you’re inspired by OPERATION CHANGE, let us know! Connect with us via social media.

Facebook (Operation Change): https://www.facebook.com/operationchng
Twitter (@operationchange): https://twitter.com/operationchange
Instagram (#operationchange): http://instagram.com/operationchange

We thank you for your support and positive contributions to the world around you!

Who can I contact if I have more questions?

If you have an unanswered question, you can reach OPERATION CHANGE via email at info@operationchange.com.
